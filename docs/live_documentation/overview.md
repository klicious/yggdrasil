# Yggdrasil: Project Overview & Vision

## Project Summary

Yggdrasil serves as the organization's living handbook and centralized knowledge repositoryâ€”the "world-tree" of operational wisdom that contains everything beyond source code. It is the single source of truth for mission statements, member biographies, organizational policies, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and high-level project dashboards. The documentation is linted, rendered as an internal MkDocs site, and indexed into a vector Knowledge Base (KB) to enable quick searching of "how we work" for both humans and AI agents.

## Vision & Goals

Yggdrasil's vision is to be the indispensable, always-current source of truth for all organizational knowledge, fostering transparency, efficiency, and a shared understanding of our collective operations. Our primary goals include:

*   **Centralize Knowledge**: Consolidate all non-code operational wisdom into a single, accessible repository.
*   **Ensure Accuracy & Currency**: Implement automated processes to keep documentation up-to-date and reflective of current practices.
*   **Enhance Discoverability**: Enable quick and efficient retrieval of information for both human users and AI agents through advanced search capabilities.
*   **Promote Collaboration**: Facilitate easy contribution and maintenance of knowledge by all team members.
*   **Support Strategic Decision-Making**: Provide a clear and comprehensive overview of organizational processes and policies to inform leadership.

## Key Stakeholders

Yggdrasil primarily benefits:

*   **All Employees**: By providing a central hub for company policies, processes, and team information.
*   **New Hires**: By offering a structured onboarding resource to quickly understand organizational culture and operations.
*   **Leadership**: By providing high-level project overviews and strategic insights.
*   **AI Agents**: By serving as a structured and searchable knowledge base for AI-driven operations and analysis.
